03 — About

Who I Am

I've seen the product experience from every angle

I'm a UX professional with a background in research, writing, and customer success — which means I've sat on every side of the table. I've written the support articles, researched why users couldn't find them, redesigned the platform they lived on, and later worked directly with the customers who rely on them every day.

I studied Writing-Intensive English at Marquette University (with extensive coursework in education), which gave me a foundation I use constantly: the ability to make complex things clear and to meet people where they are. In my UX career at Zywave, I served as both UX Writer and Digital Support Lead — essentially the product owner for the entire support experience — where I led research, shaped content strategy, and drove a platform migration that's still delivering results five years later.

I think of myself as a translator. I'm at my best when I'm helping engineers, designers, product managers, and stakeholders understand each other — and more importantly, understand the people they're building for. I love solving problems, and I love the moment when research makes the right decision feel obvious to everyone in the room.

When I'm not working, you'll probably find me cooking something ambitious, hunting for a great new restaurant, mountain biking, running, traveling, working on our home with my wife, playing with our cats, or customizing just about anything I can get my hands on.
